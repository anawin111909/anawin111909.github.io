I have been passionate about video games since childhood, which inspired me to pursue a career in game development. I later completed a Master’s degree in Game Development and Design in the UK, where I focused on creating interactive game experiences.

During my studies, I developed several game prototypes using Unity and C#, including VR and AR projects. After completing my studies, I continued to study game testing and quality assurance in more depth. I applied testing methods such as test cases, checklists , and bug reporting while testing the games I developed in my own projects, gaining hands-on experience in identifying issues and improving gameplay quality.
